Frequenly Asked Questions


1. Why is this a paid service?



Your CareConnect have been vetted for empathy and have an affinity towards elderlies, but like any other graduate, they would like to feel secure about their finances and do their best for you. To give them dignified, respectable salaries for their time invested is a paid service. This also ensures their longstanding association with us in order to fulfill an enduring companionship with you.


2. How are the CareConnect and Grandpals matched?



Your CareConnect are young, educated graduates from different backgrounds, who have been vetted for empathy using psychometric tests and background verification checks. We employ a stringent process to get to know the intent and personality of each CareConnect. We do our best to match Goodfellows with their Grandpals keeping in mind interests, backgrounds, and comfort. We will be in constant touch with you to ensure you are at ease and happy.


3. How do I get started?



We’re so glad you’re here! To get started, click on the signup button that says ‘Join The Free Trial’ and fill in the form that appears. Our team will reach out to you within 48 hours to match you with your perfect companion. If you need any assistance, please reach out to us at +91 8779524307 from Monday to Friday between 10 AM to 6 PM. We’re always here to help.


4. Are the Grandpals also checked for the safety of the young companions?

Of course! Safety from both ends is extremely important. The seniors are met by the members of the core team to assess them along with basic background checks. Also, the Goodfellows have the right to exit immediately if they feel any kind of discomfort.
